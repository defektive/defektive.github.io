Domain Takeovers

Domain Takeovers

Domain takeovers or sub-domain takeovers occur when DNS records are not properly updated or removed when a service is moved or shut down. Best case the domain just doesn’t work anymore. Worst case a threat actor can leverage 3rd party services to serve malicious content using your domain / brand.

Subsections of Domain Takeovers

Firebase

Currently Firebase protects against domain takeovers by requiring each domain to have a unique CNAME or TXT record record on the affected domain.

Squarespace

Currently Squarespace protects against domain takeovers by requiring each domain to have a unique CNAME record on the affected domain.
